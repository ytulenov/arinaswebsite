KazGazoBlock is a Kazakhstan-based manufacturer and supplier of autoclaved aerated concrete blocks, offering high-quality construction materials using modern German production technology.

## Highlights

- Negotiated and managed partnerships with 7 construction firms across Kazakhstan, expanding distribution of premium eco-friendly building materials and strengthening the brand's presence in the industry
- Implemented a structured client feedback system that surfaced recurring delivery issues, fed directly into service improvements, and measurably improved client retention
- Organized 3 factory tours showcasing the company's German production technology and quality control processes, building trust with clients and prospective partners
- Led marketing initiatives including a subscription-based discount program for repeat buyers, a proposed revamp of the company's website, and SEO enhancements to improve search visibility
